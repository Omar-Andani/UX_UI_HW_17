Omar Andani

Hey, I’m Omar. I’m a UX/UI designer from Vancouver with a degree in business. My goal is to create designs with a modern aesthetic that leave a favorable impression on people, the environment and society.


Coming from a business background, I see design as a problem-solving activity with multiple moving parts. I try to gain an understanding of each project in a holistic way and apply design principles that elevate a company’s competitive status. I value logical designs are more thanabout pixel-perfect visuals and enjoy working on projects that involve strategy just as much as visual design. 


Outside of work, I enjoy boxing, snowboarding, running with (chasing after) my 3-year-old German Shepherd, and traveling. I was fortunate to spend the last year in Hong Kong, where I met some great people and gained a unique perspective on the tech industry. This was also where I was first introduced to the field of UX/UI design.



Anything else, feel free to get in touch.
